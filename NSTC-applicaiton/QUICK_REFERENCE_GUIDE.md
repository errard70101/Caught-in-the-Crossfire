## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

1. **Start with examples** - Use your advisor's successful applications as templates
2. **Budget realistically** - Don't inflate; justify every expense
3. **Be specific** - Vague descriptions hurt your chances
4. **Show innovation** - Clearly articulate what's new/different
5. **Timeline matters** - Be realistic about what can be accomplished
6. **Proofread multiple times** - Errors reflect poorly
7. **Get feedback early** - Don't wait until the last minute
8. **Follow formats exactly** - Reviewers notice deviations
9. **Tell a story** - Make your research compelling
